Who is Jesus Christ?

He is God Almighty(Isaiah 9:6)(Isaiah 7:14 ),He is the image of the invisible God(Hebrews 1:3)(2 Corinthians 4:4),He came to save us from our sin (Matthew 1:21)(John 1:29),He is the Son of God(Psalm 2:7)(Matthew 1:18),He is both the Creator and descendant of David(Isaiah 11:1)(Matthew 1:2-26),He is the King of Kings and Lord of lords(Daniel 2:47)(Revelation 19:16),Jesus’ Father, the Spirit of God, was living within Him(John 10:30)(John 10:38),He is the baptizer with the Holy Ghost,the Comforter(Isaiah 44:3)(Isaiah 59:20-21),He is the Holy Ghost/Comforter(Matthew 28:20)(John 14:16-18 ),He will be on the throne when we arrive in Heaven(Matthew 19:28)(Revelation 3:21).
