Step One




Acknowledge and admit you have acted wrongly against God's laws. Ask yourself .Am I good person? Take the Good Test. Man is sinful nature and due to this he separated from God, so he cannot know or feel God’s Love and plan for his life.

As per Holy Bible statement: “All have sinned and fall short of the glory of God" (Romans 3:23).
Therefore man is separated from God’s companionship, According to spiritual law, the wages of sin is death (Romans 6:23), it is not physical death, its spiritual death, permanent separated from God, eternal torment. So every man needs a redeemer!
